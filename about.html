The term “MUKHWAS” has always been connected with our Indian Culture as an after meal digestive and refreshment.

All it took was an honest and humble approach of a family and their passionate desire to serve happiness in the form of traditional mukhwas. Being ahead of her times our Founder Late Smt Nirmala J Bhave was one of the very few woman entrepreneurs back then when she started preparing mukhwas using traditional recipes.Her business idea was appreciated by the locals and thus started our journey as Bhave Supari in 1957.

She was later accompanied by her son Late Shri Prasad J Bhave,who was a visionary and always believed in making Bhave Supari a household name when it came to mukhwas. He played a key role in expanding the product line by self developing various recipes and blends.

A legacy that started in 1957 as a small proprietorship “BHAVE SUPARI” has now transformed into a company namely “BHAVE FOOD PRODUCTS PVT LTD.” with the third generation leading the way.

A major challenge our organization faced was in 2013 when Govt. of Maharashtra banned manufacturing of scented betel nut (supari) within Maharashtra state thus leading us to close half of our product range. However, the organization tackled the challenge by introducing the new product range thereby retaining the original taste without including betel nut (supari).

We are proud to say that all of our products are free from betel nuts,tobacco or any such hazardous products associated with pan masala thus making it consumable for every family member. Our famous products being Masala Badishep, Masala Suvasik, Pachak and Tambul Vida.

Today we are having an automated manufacturing facility located in Satara MIDC.With a network of 25+ Distributors and over 3500+ retailers and wholesalers spread across Western Maharashtra, we have a strong setup of marketing personals. The trust our brand has gained over the 6 decades lies with the use of selected quality raw materials,adopting new techniques of manufacturing and valuing our customer feedbacks.
