Our Founders

Being ahead of her times our Founder Late Smt Nirmala J Bhave was one of very few woman entrepreneurs back then. When she started preparing mukhwas using traditional recipes, her business idea was appreciated by the locals and thus started our journey as Bhave Supari in 1957.
She was later accompanied by her son Late Shri Prasad J Bhave, was a visionary and always believed in making Bhave Supari a household name when it came to mukhwas. He played a key role in expanding the product line by self developing various recipes and blends. A legacy that started in 1957 as a small proprietorship “BHAVE SUPARI” has now transformed into a company “BHAVE FOOD PRODUCTS PVT LTD.” with the third generation leading the way
